Ishibot

Introduction

Ishibot is an autonomous mobile platform, designed with the aim of participating to the French Robotics Cup. I am currently developing multiple features for this robot: constructing the robot's frame, training an AI model for object recognition, designing the power and motor control boards. I am also planning to implement a ROS (Robot Operating System) architecture on a Raspberry Pi and integrate additional actuators.

Mechanics

Afterward, the electronic design phase comes into play. I made the decision to use an Arduino Nano as the central processor. The next step involves soldering a perfboard to accommodate the Arduino, a power converter, four servo motors, a battery, and a switch. The choice of a perfboard is deliberate, as it provides a flexible platform for potential future expansions. This includes the incorporation of additional modules such as an ultrasonic sensor or a Bluetooth module.
